Hello! I'm a senior undergraduate interesting in the math behind human decision-making and how it changes under uncertainty. Currently, I'm interning at the [AlphaGrep Quantitative Research Lab](https://wsai.iitm.ac.in/research/alpha-research-lab/) at IIT Madras, researching the effect of human cognition on investments in skewed stocks. I'm also an incoming research intern with the [IRIS](https://nusgs.nus.edu.sg/irisnus/#) Fellowship at the National University of Singapore, working under the supervision of Prof Tan Zhi Xuan on modeling imperfect human behavior.
