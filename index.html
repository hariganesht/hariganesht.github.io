Hariganesh Tangirala

Hello! I am a third year undergraduate interested in using Bayesian and probabilistic modeling to understand human incentives and decision-making, and how they change under resource constraints.

I'm currently wrapping up my engineering coursework in India. I'm also an incoming research intern with the IRIS@NUS program at the NUS School of Computing for Fall 2026, where I will work under the supervision of Prof. Tan Zhi Xuan on modeling boundedly rational decision-making in humans.

research interests

Bayesian cognition

Probabilistic models of perception, learning, and reasoning. How do minds represent and update beliefs?

Probabilistic programming

Languages for expressing generative models — WebPPL, Gen.jl, Pyro, and the inference engines underneath.

Decision making

Normative and descriptive accounts of choice; reinforcement learning; resource-rational agents.

Computational modeling

Building and fitting process-level models to behavioral data; model comparison; identifiability.
