Computational cognitive science.

I'm an undergraduate in electronics & communication engineering at VIT Vellore, currently doing research at the AlphaGrep Quantitative Research Lab at IIT Madras.

I'm interested in computational cognitive science — Bayesian models of cognition, probabilistic programming, and resource-rational decision making — and in how those ideas play out in real decision behaviour, including in financial markets. This year I'm joining the NUS CoSI lab for a research internship, and I'm aiming for a PhD in computational cognitive science.

I keep a notebook of writing where I work through ideas as I learn them. Outside research I'm into competitive debate.
